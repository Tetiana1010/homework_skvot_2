Nymph

This sculpture is a Roman copy of a Greek nymph from the group Invitation to the dance. Reconstruction by W. Klein and G.E. Rizzo.

From the Heliconian Muses let us begin to sing, who hold the great and holy mount of Helicon, and dance on soft feet about the deep-blue spring…
(Hesiod, Theogony, 1-3)
